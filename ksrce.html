Welcome to KSRCE

Chairman

Lion Dr. K. S. Rangasamy, MJF.

Greetings! I welcome you all whole-heartedly to our institution. The K.S R. College of Engineering was started with the aim of imparting quality education to all. We give top priority to discipline and knowledge. The Institution provides the necessary infrastructure and a team of qualified and motivated faculty all under one roof for the student community.

The Management

The Management

Greetings! I welcome you all whole-heartedly to our institution. The K.S R. College of Engineering was started with the aim of imparting quality education to all. We give top priority to discipline and knowledge. The Institution provides the necessary infrastructure and a team of qualified and motivated faculty all under one roof for the student community.

Secretary

Thiru. R. Srinivasan, B.B.M., MISTE

The technical colleges under Aarthi Educational & Charitable Trust are the product of a vision of a dream of excellence in affordable education. The chasm to synchronize the traditional value system and the contemporary ideas of engineering and technology is increasing at an alarming pace.

The Trust

The Trust

The KSR Educational & Charitable Trust was founded in 1984 by Lion Dr. K. S. Rangasamy, a well-known philanthropist. He hails from an agricultural family. In addition to agriculture he started doing business in distributing milk to societies. In due course, he concentrated on textile business. He developed KSR textile mills Pvt. Ltd, KSR exports and a fleet of power looms.
